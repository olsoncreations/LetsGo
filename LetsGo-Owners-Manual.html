Section 1

What Is LetsGo?

A discovery + rewards platform where users get paid to explore their city.

LetsGo is a progressive cash-back discovery platform. Users find restaurants, bars, activities, and events — then earn increasing rewards every time they visit. The more loyal a customer is, the more they earn back.

The Core Loop

1 Discover

Users browse local restaurants and activities through an engaging swipe feed, date-night generator, voting games, event calendar, and social content feed.

2 Visit

Users go to the business, enjoy their experience, and keep their receipt.

3 Upload

Users upload their receipt via the Profile page. They snap a photo, enter the total, and submit for approval.

4 Earn

Once approved, users earn progressive cash-back — starting at 3% and increasing up to possibly as high as 50% with repeated visits to the same business.

Who Uses LetsGo?

Customers (Users)

  • Discover new restaurants, bars, and activities
  • Play games to decide where to go
  • Earn real cash-back on every visit
  • Share experiences with the community
  • Track earnings and visit history

Businesses (Partners)

  • Get discovered by local customers
  • Build loyal, repeat visitors
  • Set their own reward tiers
  • Manage receipts and media
  • Run advertising campaigns
Key Selling Point Unlike traditional loyalty programs, LetsGo rewards increase with every visit. A first-time visitor could earn 3%, but a 60+ visit regular could earn up to as high as 50%. This creates a powerful incentive to keep coming back to the same business.

How Rewards Work

Progressive cash-back that grows with every visit.

Progressive Payout Tiers

Every business on LetsGo has 7 payout tiers. As a user visits the same business more, they unlock higher reward percentages.

Tier Name Visits Cash-Back % Visual
1 Starter 1 – 10 5.0%  
2 Regular 11 – 20 7.5%  
3 Favorite 21 – 30 10.0%  
4 VIP 31 – 40 12.5%  
5 Elite 41 – 50 15.0%  
6 Legend 51 – 60 17.5%  
7 Ultimate 61+ 20.0%  
How It's Calculated Payout = Receipt Subtotal × Tier Percentage
Example: A $50 receipt subtotal at Tier 3 (10%) = $5.00 cash-back to the user.
Example: A $80 receipt subtotal at Tier 7 (20%) = $16.00 cash-back to the user.

The 365-Day Rolling Window

Visit counts are tracked on a 365-day rolling window per user based on the user's signup anniversary. This means:

  • Only visits within the last 365 days count toward the tier
  • Each user/business relationship is tracked independently
  • Every year, on the user's anniversary, their Progressive Payout Tiers will reset to zero
  • Only approved receipts count toward visit totals

Example Journey

Sarah discovers "Taco Palace" on LetsGo:

  • Visit 1: $30 dinner → 5% payout = $1.50 earned
  • Visit 5: $45 dinner → still Tier 1 (5%) = $2.25 earned
  • Visit 11: $30 dinner → now Tier 2 (7.5%) = $2.25 earned
  • Visit 25: $50 dinner → now Tier 3 (10%) = $5.00 earned
  • Visit 61: $50 dinner → Tier 7 (20%) = $10.00 earned!

Sarah has become a loyal regular. The increasing rewards kept her coming back, and now she earns top-tier cash-back at her favorite taco spot.

5v3v1 — Full Game Flow

Game Setup

  1. Basics
    Name the game session and choose a category (Restaurant, Bar, Activity, Nightclub, Coffee Shop, etc.). Set your location.
  2. Rounds
    The game automatically structures into 3 rounds: 5 options → 3 options → 1 winner.
  3. Rules
    Configure filters — distance, price level, tags, and whether to show only open businesses. Hidden voting can be toggled on/off.
  4. Invite
    Share a game code with a friend. They enter the code to join the session.

Playing the Game

Round 1: Pick 5

The system presents businesses matching the filters. Each player votes on their top 5 picks. Votes are tallied.

Round 2: Narrow to 3

The top 5 voted businesses are shown. Players now pick their top 3. The funnel narrows.

Round 3: Final Pick

The remaining 3 businesses are shown. Each player picks their #1. The winner is revealed with full business details.

Additional Features

  • 7 Waiting Room Mini-Games — Fun games to play while waiting for your partner to join or vote
  • Winner Celebration — Animated reveal of the winning business with full details, address, hours, and payout tiers
  • Game History — Expandable cards showing past games with business details and "Would Go Again" hearts
  • Discovery Links — Jump from game results to the full Discovery Feed entry for any business
Sales Tip The 5v3v1 game is a powerful engagement tool. Every time friends play, businesses get exposed to potential customers through a fun, social experience. Businesses appearing in games get organic discovery.

Section 16

Revenue & Fee Structure

How LetsGo generates revenue from business partners.

Business Packages

Choose Your Package — Basic vs Premium
Two Separate Charges Every approved receipt generates two charges for the business. These are completely independent calculations. Do not confuse them.

1. User Payout (Business → LetsGo → User)

What: Progressive cash-back the business pays through LetsGo, which LetsGo then distributes to the user.

Amount: Determined by the business's payout tier plan (3% – 50%)

Cap: No per-receipt cap. The business's chosen tier percentage applies in full.

Formula: payout = receipt_subtotal × tier_percent

2. LetsGo Platform Fee (Business → LetsGo)

What: Platform fee that LetsGo charges the business for each processed receipt. This is LetsGo's revenue.

Amount: 10% of receipt subtotal

Cap: $5.00 maximum per receipt

Formula: fee = min(receipt_subtotal × 10%, $5.00)

Cost Example for Businesses

Basic Plan — Monthly Cost Estimate

Assume a restaurant processes 200 receipts/month with an average ticket of $40:

  • Customers @Tier 1 (5%): 150 receipts × $38 × 5% = $285
  • Customers @Tier 2 (7.5%): 30 receipts × $40 × 7.5% = $90
  • Customers @Tier 3 (10%+): 20 receipts × $55 × 10% = $110
  • Total User Payouts: ~$485/month

Plus LetsGo Platform Fee:

  • Customers @Tier 1: 150 receipts × $38 × 10% = $570
  • Customers @Tier 2: 30 receipts × $40 × 10% = $120
  • Customers @Tier 3: 20 receipts × $5 (reached $5 cap) = $100
  • Total Platform Fee: ~$790/month

Total Business Cost under Basic Plan: ~$485 + $790 = $1,275 or $6.38 avg per user

Premium Plan — Monthly Cost Estimate

Assume a restaurant processes 200 receipts/month with an average ticket of $40:

  • Customers @Tier 1 (5%): 150 receipts × $38 × 5% = $285
  • Customers @Tier 2 (7.5%): 30 receipts × $40 × 7.5% = $90
  • Customers @Tier 3 (10%+): 20 receipts × $55 × 10% = $110
  • Total User Payouts: ~$485/month

Plus LetsGo Platform Fee:

  • Premium monthly subscription: $399.99/month
  • No per-receipt platform fee (10% fee is waived)
  • Total Platform Fee: $399.99/month

Total Business Cost under Premium Plan: ~$485 + $399.99 = $884.99 or $4.43 avg per user

Sales Talking Point The $5 cap on the platform fee is a big selling point for businesses with higher average tickets. A $200 dinner only costs $5 in platform fees — that's just 2.5%. The fee percentage effectively decreases as ticket sizes increase. Premium plan businesses save significantly on platform fees — at 200 receipts/month the savings are nearly $400.

Section 18

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from customers and business partners.

For Users (Customers)

How do I earn cash-back?

Visit a business on the LetsGo platform, keep your receipt, then upload it through the Profile page. Once the receipt is approved, you earn cash-back based on your current payout tier for that business.

How much can I earn?

It depends on how often you visit. First-time visitors can earn 3% (Standard plan). As you visit the same business more, your tier increases — up to possibly 50% at the Ultimate tier (61+ visits). There is no cap on user payouts.

How long does receipt approval take?

Most receipts are reviewed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification when your receipt is approved or if there's an issue.

What if my receipt is rejected?

Common reasons include: illegible receipt image, receipt from a non-partner business, duplicate submission, or date in the future. You'll see the rejection reason on your receipt history and can resubmit with a clearer photo if applicable.

Do my visits expire?

Yes, visit counts are based on a 365-day rolling window based on the user's anniversary. Every year the visits and tiers will reset to zero.

How do I get paid?

Earned cash-back accumulates in your LetsGo balance. You can set up your preferred payout method (bank transfer, etc.) in your Profile settings.

Is there a tax form?

If your total earnings exceed the IRS threshold ($600 in a calendar year), you may receive a 1099 form. You can manage your tax information in your Profile settings.

For Business Partners

How much does it cost to join?

There are two costs: (1) the user payout percentage you choose during setup (your reward to customers), and (2) based on your package, could either be the lesser of 10% receipt subtotal or $5 cap (Basic Package), or $399.99/month (Premium Package).

Can I change my payout tiers later?

Yes. You can adjust your payout structure through the Business Dashboard or by contacting LetsGo support. Changes apply to future receipts. Only 1 change is allowed every 365 days.

How do I approve receipts?

Log into your Business Dashboard and go to the Receipts tab. You'll see all pending submissions with receipt images, amounts, and user details. Approve or reject each one individually or use bulk actions.

What's the difference between Basic and Premium?

Basic includes Discovery Feed listing, 5v3v1, Group Vote, receipt processing, and payout management. Premium adds Analytics charts, Event creation, Spotlight Advertising campaigns, Date Night Generator, and enhanced UGC management tools.

How do advertising Spotlights work?

Spotlights boost your business to the top of the Discovery Feed for a set period (1 day, 7 days, or 14 days). Push campaigns send notifications directly to users in your area (100-mile radius or platform-wide). All campaigns include performance tracking.

Can I control user-generated content about my business?

Yes. All user-submitted photos and videos require your approval before appearing in the public Experiences Feed. You can approve, reject, or delete content from your Media tab.

Common Questions
"We already have a loyalty program."
LetsGo doesn't replace it — it finds customers first. Your existing program retains the ones you have. LetsGo brings new people through the door and gives them a reason to come back that stacks on top of what you already offer.
"We already use Yelp/Google Ads."
Those charge you for impressions and clicks — not results. LetsGo only charges when a customer actually visits and spends money. And unlike ads, LetsGo also brings them back a second, third, and tenth time.
"What if people just come for the rewards?"
They're still spending money at your business. The average reward is 5–10% — you keep 90–95% of every dollar. And the progressive system means the biggest rewards go to your most loyal customers, who would have come anyway.
"We can't afford another marketing tool."
There's nothing to afford upfront. Basic plan costs $0 until someone actually walks through your door. If it doesn't work, it costs you nothing. If it does work, it already paid for itself.
"How do I know the receipts are real?"
Every receipt is submitted with a photo and goes through your approval dashboard. You see the amount, the date, and the image. Nothing gets paid out until you approve it. Full control, zero fraud risk.

Frequently Asked Questions
How does the progressive payout system work?
Customers earn increasing cash-back percentages the more they visit. Starting at 4% for their first 10 visits, progressing up to 10% for 61+ visits. The payout resets on a 365-day rolling window per customer.
When do I get charged?
Billing is monthly. Your invoice includes the premium plan fee (if applicable), progressive payouts you owe to customers, and any credit card processing fees (3.5%). Basic plan businesses also pay a LetsGo platform fee (10% capped at $5) — Premium plan businesses do not. Invoices generate on the 1st of each month.
Can I dispute a customer receipt?
Yes. You have a 30-day dispute window after approving a receipt. Go to the Receipts tab, find the receipt in your history, and click the dispute button. Our team reviews within 48 hours.
How do I upgrade to Premium?
Go to the Plans & Billing tab and click the Premium plan. Premium unlocks event management, advertising campaigns, advanced analytics, unlimited media uploads, and priority support.
What's the difference between my photos and user-generated content?
Your photos appear on your discovery card and profile. User-generated content (UGC) is submitted by customers and must be approved by you before appearing on the public Experiences feed.
How do events help my business?
Events appear in the LetsGo discovery feed and event calendar, driving new visitors to your business. Users can RSVP and share events with friends, increasing your reach organically.
